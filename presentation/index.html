⚠️ The Catch

  • Robots can be confidently wrong 😬
  • They follow instructions super-literally 📏
  • They have no feelings or values — they don't know what's kind or fair ❤️⚖️
Say: "Fast is useful. Wise is better." Emphasize that values come from people, not machines.

So… WHO'S IN CHARGE?

Do the call-and-response loudly here: "Who's in charge?" Class: "WE ARE!"

💪 Your 3 Human Superpowers

  1. Set the goal — you decide what's worth doing 🎯
  2. Check the work — you catch mistakes 🕵️
  3. Decide what's right — you bring fairness and kindness ⚖️💚

A robot has none of these on its own.

Call these "human gifts". Invite one student example for each if time allows.

🌟 Robots are fast. Humans are wise.

Final shout:

"Who's in charge?" ➜ "WE ARE!"

End with confidence and applause cue. Optional: ask students one thing they will check before trusting AI.
